Real-time Development

A small project built with my 7-year-old to explore how ideas turn into something real. We used voice-to-text sessions with him as the user, capturing how he thinks, speaks, and interacts. Those sessions were turned into simple requirements and then into deliverables for AI engineering agents. It was a fast, hands-on way to test user-driven design, especially with a non-technical user, and see how raw input can shape a product from the start.
